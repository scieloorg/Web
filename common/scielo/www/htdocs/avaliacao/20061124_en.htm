New journals in SciELO Brazil collection

The 12th Meeting of the Advisory Committee of SciELO Brazil was held at BIREME/PAHO/WHO on november days 23 e 24, 2006, and approved the inclusion of 17 new Brazilian scientific journals, which will soon be available at the site SciELO Brazil.
