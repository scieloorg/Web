Figure 2. In vivo expression of nif'-'lacZ from the nifE (E) and nifH (H) promoters in the E. coli MC1061 strain in the presence (+ NifA) or absence of the NifA activator protein provided by pCK3 plasmid. Expression of nif'-'lacZ fusion was measured as a function of the OD600 for cultures grown in A medium (see Material and Methods). In each case, three data points (between OD600 of 0.2 and 0.6) were used to determine the slope of the line, which reflects the differential rate of nif'-'lacZ expression. Curve labeled MC (which overlaps the curve from the nifH promoter in the absence of NifA) gives data for the E. coli MC1061 host strain (negative control).
